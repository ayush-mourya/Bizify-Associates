Labour Law Compliance Services

Adhering to India's vast and dynamic labour laws is crucial for maintaining a healthy employer-employee relationship and avoiding legal disputes. Bizify Associates provides expert guidance and support for compliance with various labour legislations, including the Minimum Wages Act, Payment of Wages Act, Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, and more. We help businesses establish robust HR practices, conduct audits, and ensure all statutory requirements related to employment are met.

Documents Required (Varies by specific law)

  • Employee records (appointment letters, service records)
  • Attendance and leave records
  • Payroll records and salary registers
  • ESI & PF challans and returns
  • Shop & Establishment Act registration certificate
  • Compliance registers as per various acts
  • Details of contractors (if applicable)
