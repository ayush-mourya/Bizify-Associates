Shop & Establishment Act Compliance

The Shop and Establishment Act is a state-specific regulation governing the working conditions of employees in shops, commercial establishments, restaurants, and other non-factory workplaces. Bizify Associates assists businesses with obtaining the necessary registration, maintaining prescribed records, adhering to rules regarding working hours, holidays, and employment conditions. Ensuring compliance is vital for smooth operations and avoiding legal issues specific to your state.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Aadhaar Card of the Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Proof of establishment address (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, NOC)
  • Details of employees (if any)
  • Nature of business activity
  • Commencement date of business
  • Identity and address proof of the applicant
