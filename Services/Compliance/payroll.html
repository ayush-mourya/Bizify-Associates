Payroll Management Services

Efficient payroll management is vital for any business, ensuring timely and accurate salary disbursement while complying with all statutory deductions and filings. Bizify Associates offers comprehensive payroll services, including salary processing, TDS deductions, ESI and PF contributions, generation of payslips, and annual tax statements. We streamline your payroll process, reduce administrative burden, and ensure adherence to all labor laws, allowing you to focus on your core business operations.

Documents Required

  • Employee Data (Name, Bank Account, PAN, Aadhaar)
  • Salary Structure details for each employee
  • Attendance records
  • Details of any deductions or reimbursements
  • Company's PF and ESIC registration details
  • Professional Tax registration details (if applicable)
  • Previous payroll records (for handover)
