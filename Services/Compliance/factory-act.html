Factory Act Compliance

The Factories Act, 1948, sets out comprehensive provisions for the health, safety, welfare, and working conditions of workers in factories across India. Bizify Associates helps manufacturing units ensure full compliance with this Act, covering aspects like factory registration, maintenance of registers and records, provisions for health and safety, working hours, and annual returns. Our services minimize risks of non-compliance and foster a safe working environment.

Documents Required

  • Factory Plan and Site Plan
  • Details of machinery installed
  • Consent to Establish and Operate from Pollution Control Board
  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of owners/directors
  • Proof of ownership/occupancy of premises
  • Details of manufacturing process
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from Fire Department
