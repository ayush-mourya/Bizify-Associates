ESIC Registration & Compliance

Employee's State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) is a crucial social security scheme for Indian workers, providing medical, maternity, and disablement benefits. Bizify Associates assists businesses with ESIC registration, monthly contribution filing, and ensuring complete compliance with the ESI Act. We help you navigate the complexities of ESIC regulations, ensuring your employees receive their due benefits and your business avoids penalties.

Documents Required

  • Certificate of Registration/Incorporation (Company/LLP)
  • PAN Card of the establishment and directors/partners
  • Address proof of the establishment
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association (MOA/AOA) or Partnership Deed
  • Bank account details of the company
  • List of employees with their details (name, father's name, DOB, salary, Aadhaar, PAN)
  • Copy of current bank statement
  • Cancelled cheque of the company's bank account
