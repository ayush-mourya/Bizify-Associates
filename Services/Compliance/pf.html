Provident Fund (PF) Registration & Compliance

Employee Provident Fund (PF) is a mandatory savings scheme for salaried employees in India, managed by the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO). Bizify Associates offers comprehensive services for PF registration, monthly challan generation and payment, UAN activation, and all related PF compliance requirements. We ensure your business adheres to all EPF regulations, contributing to your employees' financial security and maintaining your legal standing.

Documents Required

  • Certificate of Incorporation/Registration
  • PAN Card of the establishment
  • Address proof of the establishment
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) of a director/partner
  • List of employees with their details (name, father's name, DOB, salary, Aadhaar, PAN)
  • Specimen signature of authorized signatory
