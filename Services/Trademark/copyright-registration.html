Copyright Registration

Copyright registration protects original literary, dramatic, musical, and artistic works, including software and cinematograph films. It grants creators exclusive rights to reproduce, distribute, perform, or display their work, preventing unauthorized use and infringement. Bizify Associates guides you through the entire copyright registration process in India.

Documents Required

  • Applicant's Name, Address, and Nationality
  • Nature of Applicant's Interest in the work (e.g., author, publisher)
  • Title of the work and Language of the work
  • Name, address, and nationality of the author/creator
  • Year of first publication (if published)
  • Information on prior publications (if any)
  • NOC from the publisher (if applicant is not publisher)
  • NOC from persons whose photographs appear in the work
  • Two copies of the work (book, script, music, art, software code)
  • Power of Attorney (if applying through an agent)
