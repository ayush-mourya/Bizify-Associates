Trademark Registration & IP Protection

A trademark helps protect brand identity and goodwill by registering unique logos, word-marks or tag-lines with the Controller General of Patents, Designs & Trademarks. Successful registration grants exclusive rights for 10 years (renewable) and defends against infringement, objections, and oppositions, securing your intellectual property.

Documents Required

  • Signed Form TM‑A (Application for Trademark Registration)
  • Applicant's Identity Proof (PAN, Aadhaar, Passport)
  • Applicant's Address Proof (Utility Bill, Bank Statement)
  • Certificate of Incorporation (for Company/LLP) or Partnership Deed
  • Udyog Aadhaar or DPIIT/Startup Certificate (if applicable)
  • High-resolution JPEG or PNG file of the Trademark/Logo (if applicable)
  • Signed Form TM‑48 (Power of Attorney in favor of the trademark agent/attorney)
  • (If claiming priority) Certified copy of the foreign application + English translation
  • User affidavit (if claiming prior use of the trademark)
