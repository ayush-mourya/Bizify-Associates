Trademark Objection Reply

A Trademark Objection is raised by the Trademark Examiner if they find issues with a trademark application during examination. This could be due to similar existing marks, descriptive nature, or other grounds.Bizify Associates helps you draft and file a robust reply to overcome such objections, increasing your chances of successful trademark registration.

Documents Required

  • Copy of the Trademark Examination Report/Objection Notice
  • Trademark Application Number
  • Applicant's Name and Contact Details
  • Details of prior use of the trademark (if any, with evidence)
  • Supporting documents or affidavits to justify the uniqueness of the mark
  • Any existing trademark search reports
  • Power of Attorney (if applying through an agent)
