Patent Registration

Patent registration grants exclusive rights to an invention, preventing others from making, using, selling, or importing it without the patent holder's permission for a limited period (20 years in India). Bizify Associates assists innovators and businesses in navigating the complex process of patent search, filing, and prosecution to protect their groundbreaking innovations.

Documents Required

  • Application Form (Form 1)
  • Provisional/Complete Specification (Form 2)
  • Statement and Undertaking (Form 3)
  • Declaration as to Inventorship (Form 5)
  • Proof of right to apply for patent (e.g., assignment deed)
  • Drawings or illustrations of the invention (if applicable)
  • Abstract of the invention
  • Power of Attorney (if applying through an agent)
  • Priority document (if claiming priority from an earlier application)
  • Details of related applications (if any)
