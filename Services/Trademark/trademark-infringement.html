Trademark Infringement & Enforcement

Trademark infringement occurs when an unauthorized party uses a trademark that is identical or confusingly similar to a registered trademark, causing confusion among consumers. Bizify Associates offers services to monitor, detect, and take legal action against trademark infringement, including sending cease and desist notices and pursuing litigation to protect your brand rights.

Documents Required

  • Copy of your Registered Trademark Certificate
  • Evidence of infringement (e.g., screenshots, product photos, invoices)
  • Details of the infringing party (if known)
  • Proof of goodwill and reputation of your trademark (e.g., advertising, sales figures)
  • Communication history with the infringing party (if any)
  • Applicant/Company details (PAN, Aadhaar, Incorporation Certificate)
  • Power of Attorney (if applying through an agent)
