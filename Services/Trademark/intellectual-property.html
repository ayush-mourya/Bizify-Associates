Intellectual Property Advisory & Management

Intellectual Property (IP) refers to creations of the mind, such as inventions, literary and artistic works, designs, symbols, names, and images used in commerce. Bizify Associates offers comprehensive IP advisory services, helping businesses identify, protect, manage, and enforce their intellectual assets, including trademarks, copyrights, and patents.

Documents Required (General IP Consultation)

  • Nature of the intellectual property (e.g., idea, invention, brand name)
  • Any existing documentation related to the IP (e.g., designs, prototypes, codes)
  • Company/Applicant details (PAN, Aadhaar, Incorporation Certificate)
  • Details of creators/inventors
  • Any previous attempts at registration or protection
  • Specific concerns or goals related to IP protection
