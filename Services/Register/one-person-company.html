One Person Company (OPC) Registration

The One Person Company (OPC) concept allows a single entrepreneur to establish a company with limited liability, offering the benefits of incorporation without the need for multiple shareholders or directors. Bizify Associates specializes in OPC registration, guiding you through the entire process, including obtaining DSC and DIN, name reservation, drafting Memorandum and Articles of Association (MOA/AOA), and filing with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA).

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the sale Director & Shareholder
  • Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/Passport/Driving License of the sale Director & Shareholder
  • Proof of Registered Office (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, NOC)
  • Bank Statement/Utility Bill of the sale Director & Shareholder (latest)
  • Passport size photograph of the sale Director & Shareholder
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) and DIN
  • Nominee Consent (PAN and Aadhaar of Nominee)
