Partnership Firm Registration

A Partnership Firm is a popular business structure where two or more individuals agree to share the profits of a business carried on by all or any of them acting for all. While registration of a partnership firm is not mandatory, it is highly recommended for legal recognition and ease of doing business. Bizify Associates assists with drafting comprehensive Partnership Deeds, outlining terms and conditions, and facilitating the registration of your firm with the Registrar of Firms, providing a strong legal foundation for your collaboration.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the Partnership Firm
  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of all Partners
  • Proof of Registered Office (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, NOC)
  • Partnership Deed (drafting assistance provided)
  • Passport size photographs of all Partners
  • Bank Statement/Cancelled Cheque of Partners
  • Affidavits declaring intention to form a partnership
