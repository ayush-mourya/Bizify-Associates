Shop & Industry Specific Registrations

Beyond standard company structures, many businesses require specific registrations based on their nature of operations, whether it's a retail shop, a service establishment, or an industrial unit. Bizify Associates assists with obtaining the necessary state-specific Shop & Establishment Act registrations, as well as other industry-specific registrations and permits vital for legal and compliant operation. We simplify the process of understanding and securing these crucial licenses.

Documents Required (Varies by State/Industry)

  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Proof of ownership/tenancy of the premises (Rent Agreement, Sale Deed)
  • Electricity Bill of the premises
  • Nature of business activity
  • List of employees (if any)
  • Bank Statement/Cancelled Cheque of business account
