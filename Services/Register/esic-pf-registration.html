ESIC & PF Registration Services

For businesses with employees, **ESIC (Employee's State Insurance Corporation)** and **PF (Provident Fund)** registrations are mandatory social security compliances. Bizify Associates provides comprehensive assistance for both ESIC and PF registration processes, ensuring your business adheres to the legal requirements for employee welfare benefits. We streamline the application and documentation, setting up your compliance foundation correctly from the start.

Documents Required (Combined)

  • Certificate of Registration/Incorporation of business
  • PAN Card of the establishment and partners/directors
  • Address proof of the establishment
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque of the company
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) of authorized signatory
  • List of employees with their details (Name, Aadhaar, PAN, Salary)
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association (for company) / Partnership Deed (for LLP/Partnership)
