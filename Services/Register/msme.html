MSME (Udyam) Registration

Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) are the backbone of the Indian economy. Registering your business under the Udyam Registration portal (formerly Udyog Aadhaar) allows you to avail various government schemes, subsidies, and benefits designed to promote and support MSMEs. Bizify Associates assists businesses with quick and hassle-free MSME Udyam registration, helping you unlock growth opportunities, access credit facilities, and enjoy preference in government tenders.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Number of the applicant
  • PAN of the enterprise (mandatory from 01.04.2021)
  • GSTIN (if available, not mandatory for all)
  • Bank account details (Account Number, IFSC Code)
  • Details of investment in Plant & Machinery/Equipment
  • Details of Turnover
  • Nature of business activity (NIC Code)
