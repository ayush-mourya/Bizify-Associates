Sole Proprietorship Registration

Sole Proprietorship is the simplest form of business structure in India, owned and managed by a single individual. It's easy to set up and has minimal compliance requirements, making it ideal for small businesses and individual professionals. While there's no specific registration for a sole proprietorship, Bizify Associates helps you establish its legal identity by assisting with essential registrations like GST registration, Shop & Establishment Act license, or MSME Udyam Registration, thereby formalizing your business operations.

Documents Required (for related registrations)

  • PAN Card of the Proprietor
  • Aadhaar Card of the Proprietor
  • Proof of business address (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, NOC)
  • Bank account details (for business if applicable)
  • Nature of business activity
  • Passport size photograph of the Proprietor
