Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) Registration

A Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) combines the benefits of a company and a partnership, offering limited liability to its partners while allowing flexibility in its operation. Bizify Associates provides expert assistance in LLP registration, from obtaining DPIN and DSC for partners to drafting the LLP agreement and filing the necessary forms with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). We ensure a smooth and compliant registration process for your LLP.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of all Designated Partners
  • Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/Passport/Driving License of all Designated Partners
  • Proof of Registered Office (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, NOC)
  • Bank Statement/Utility Bill of Designated Partners (latest)
  • Passport size photographs of Designated Partners
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) and DPIN
  • LLP Agreement (drafting assistance provided)
