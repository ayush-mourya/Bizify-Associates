Trust & NGO Registration

For organizations focused on social welfare, charitable activities, or non-profit objectives, registering as a Trust or Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) is the appropriate legal pathway. Bizify Associates provides expert assistance for the registration of Public Charitable Trusts and Societies under the Societies Registration Act. We guide you through the documentation, drafting of trust deeds/memorandums, and filing processes to establish your non-profit entity correctly and compliantly.

Documents Required (for Trust/Society)

  • ID proof & Address proof of Settlor/Founders and Trustees/Governing Body members
  • PAN Card of Settlor/Founders and Trustees/Governing Body members
  • Registered office address proof (Electricity Bill, NOC)
  • Draft Trust Deed/Memorandum of Association & Rules and Regulations
  • Object clause of the Trust/Society
  • Minimum number of members/trustees as per act
