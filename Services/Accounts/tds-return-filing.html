TDS Return Filing

Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is a system where tax is deducted at the point of income generation. Businesses and individuals liable to deduct TDS must file quarterly TDS returns with the Income Tax Department. Bizify Associates provides comprehensive TDS return filing services, ensuring accurate calculation, timely payment, and compliance with all Income Tax regulations to avoid penalties.

Documents Required

  • PAN and TAN of the deductor (your company/individual)
  • PAN and Aadhaar of the deductees (those from whom tax is deducted)
  • Nature of payment and amount paid/credited
  • Date of payment/credit and date of TDS deduction
  • Challan details for TDS payment
  • Bank statement reflecting TDS payments
  • Previous TDS return acknowledgment (if applicable)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or Aadhaar OTP for filing
