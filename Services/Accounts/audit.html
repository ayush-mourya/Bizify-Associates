Audit Services

Auditing involves an independent examination of financial records to ensure their accuracy, fairness, and compliance with accounting standards and regulations. For many businesses, particularly companies, statutory audits are mandatory. Bizify Associates provides comprehensive audit services, including statutory audit, internal audit, and tax audit, helping businesses maintain transparency, improve financial controls, and fulfill regulatory obligations.

Documents Required (General Audit)

  • Books of Accounts (Ledgers, Journals, Cash Book, Bank Book)
  • Bank Statements and Bank Reconciliation Statements
  • Sales and Purchase Invoices
  • Expense Vouchers and Supporting Documents
  • Fixed Asset Register
  • Loan Agreements and Repayment Schedules
  • Statutory Registrations (GST, PAN, TAN, Incorporation Certificate)
  • Previous Year's Audit Report and Financial Statements
  • Board Meeting Minutes and General Meeting Minutes
  • Employee Records and Payroll Details
