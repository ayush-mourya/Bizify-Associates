Annual Compliance for Businesses

Annual compliance involves adhering to all legal and regulatory requirements that businesses must fulfill each financial year. This includes filing annual returns with ROC, maintaining statutory registers, conducting board meetings, and preparing financial statements. Bizify Associates ensures your company meets all its annual compliance obligations, helping you avoid penalties and maintain good standing with regulatory bodies like MCA.

Documents Required (General)

  • Financial Statements (Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Account)
  • Audit Report (if applicable)
  • Board Reports and Annexures
  • Details of Directors and Shareholders (DIN, PAN, Aadhaar)
  • Bank Statements
  • GST Returns filed for the year
  • Income Tax Returns filed for the year
  • Fixed Asset Register
  • Loan Agreements and details (if any)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) of directors/authorized signatory
