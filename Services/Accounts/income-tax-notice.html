Income Tax Notice & Scrutiny Reply

Receiving an Income Tax notice can be daunting. These notices are issued for various reasons, including discrepancies in filed returns, non-filing, or scrutiny of transactions. Bizify Associates offers expert assistance in understanding the notice, preparing accurate responses, compiling necessary documents, and representing you before tax authorities to ensure a smooth resolution and compliance.

Documents Required

  • Copy of the Income Tax Notice received
  • PAN and Aadhaar of the assessee
  • Income Tax Return (ITR) for the relevant assessment year
  • Bank statements for the relevant period
  • Proof of income sources (salary slips, business accounts)
  • Investment proofs, if applicable
  • Documents related to specific queries raised in the notice
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for online response
