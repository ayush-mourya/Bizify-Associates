GST Registration

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a unified indirect tax system in India, replacing multiple indirect taxes. Businesses exceeding a certain turnover threshold or engaged in specific activities are mandated to register under GST. Bizify Associates simplifies the GST registration process, ensuring you obtain your GSTIN seamlessly and comply with all legal requirements.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the applicant entity (Proprietor/Company/LLP/Partnership Firm)
  • Aadhaar Card of Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Proof of business registration (Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed, etc.)
  • Proof of address of business premises (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, Sale Deed)
  • Bank account details (Bank Statement, Cancelled Cheque)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or Aadhaar OTP for E-sign
  • Authorization letter/Board Resolution for authorized signatory
  • Nature of business activity
