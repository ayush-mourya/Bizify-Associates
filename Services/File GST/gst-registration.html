GST Registration

Goods and Services Tax (GST) registration is mandatory for businesses exceeding a certain turnover threshold or engaged in specific types of supply. Obtaining a Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN) allows businesses to collect GST, claim input tax credit, and ensures legal compliance. Bizify Associates provides end-to-end assistance with hassle-free GST registration, making your business GST-ready.

Documents Required

  • PAN of the Applicant (Company/Individual/LLP)
  • Aadhaar Card of the Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Proof of business registration (e.g., Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed)
  • Address proof of the principal place of business (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, Property Tax Receipt)
  • Bank account details (Account number, IFSC code, Bank name)
  • Authorized Signatory details (PAN, Aadhaar, Proof of Appointment)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for Company/LLP
  • Nature of business activities
