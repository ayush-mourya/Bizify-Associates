GST Filing

GST (Goods and Services Tax) filing involves submitting various returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.) to report outward supplies, inward supplies, and pay tax liabilities. Regular and accurate GST filing is crucial for businesses to maintain compliance, claim input tax credit, and avoid penalties. Bizify Associates provides end-to-end GST filing services, ensuring timely and error-free submissions.

Documents Required

  • GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number)
  • Sales data (Outward Supplies): Invoices issued, Credit/Debit notes
  • Purchase data (Inward Supplies): Invoices received, Credit/Debit notes
  • Details of E-way bills generated
  • Bank statements (for reconciliation)
  • Details of advance receipts and adjustments
  • Details of HSN/SAC codes for goods/services
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or Aadhaar OTP for E-sign
  • Login credentials for GST Portal
