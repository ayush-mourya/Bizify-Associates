GST Return Filing

Under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime in India, businesses are required to file various periodic returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, etc.) to declare their sales, purchases, and input tax credit. Timely and accurate GST filing is crucial to avoid penalties and ensure smooth business operations. Bizify Associates simplifies your GST compliance by handling all aspects of return preparation and filing.

Documents Required

  • GSTIN of the taxpayer
  • Sales invoices/outward supply details (B2B, B2C, Exports)
  • Purchase invoices/inward supply details (for Input Tax Credit)
  • Details of credit/debit notes issued/received
  • Bank statements (for payment reconciliation)
  • HSN/SAC codes for goods/services supplied
  • Previous month's GST returns (if applicable)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or Aadhaar OTP for filing
