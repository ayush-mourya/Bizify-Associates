Reply to GST Notice

GST notices can be issued for various reasons, including discrepancies in returns, short payment of tax, or non-compliance. Ignoring a GST notice can lead to significant penalties. Bizify Associates provides expert assistance in analyzing your GST notice, preparing a detailed and accurate reply, gathering supporting documents, and ensuring timely submission to the authorities to resolve issues and maintain compliance.

Documents Required

  • Copy of the GST Notice received
  • GSTIN of the taxpayer
  • Relevant GST Returns filed (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, etc.)
  • Sales and Purchase Registers/Invoices for the period in question
  • Bank Statements for reconciliation
  • Proof of GST payments (Challans)
  • Any other relevant documents to support your response
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for online submission
