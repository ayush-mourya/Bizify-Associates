GST Registration Cancellation & Revocation

Businesses may need to cancel their GST registration if they cease operations, their turnover falls below the threshold, or for other reasons. If a registration is cancelled by the authorities (e.g., for non-filing of returns), businesses can apply for revocation. Bizify Associates assists with both GST cancellation requests and the complex process of revocation applications, ensuring all legal formalities are met.

Documents Required (for Cancellation/Revocation)

  • GSTIN of the taxpayer
  • Reason for cancellation (e.g., closure of business, turnover below threshold)
  • Date from which cancellation is sought
  • Details of final returns (GSTR-10) to be filed
  • Input Tax Credit reversal details (if any)
  • Bank account details
  • Documents supporting the reason for cancellation/revocation
  • Any notices received regarding cancellation
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or Aadhaar OTP for filing
