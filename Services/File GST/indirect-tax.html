Indirect Tax Advisory & Compliance

Indirect taxes, like Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India, are levied on goods and services rather than directly on income. Businesses must navigate complex GST laws, including registration, invoicing, input tax credit, and regular return filing. Bizify Associates provides expert indirect tax advisory and compliance services to ensure seamless operations and minimize tax liabilities.

Documents Required (General for Indirect Tax Advice)

  • Nature of Business Operations and Services/Goods provided
  • GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Sales and Purchase Registers
  • Invoices issued and received
  • Details of input tax credit claimed
  • Any specific tax notices or queries received
  • Existing tax compliance records (e.g., old VAT, Service Tax)
  • Company/Individual PAN and Aadhaar
