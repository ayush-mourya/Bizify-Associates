Udyam (MSME/SSI) Registration

Udyam Registration, formerly known as SSI or MSME Registration, is a government certification for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in India. It enables businesses to avail various benefits and schemes offered by the Ministry of MSME, including priority sector lending, excise exemption schemes, and easier access to credit. Bizify Associates facilitates a smooth and quick online Udyam registration process.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Number of the proprietor/managing partner/director
  • PAN of the Enterprise
  • GSTIN (if available, mandatory for enterprises with GST registration)
  • Business Name and Type of Organization
  • Address of the plant/business operation
  • Date of Commencement of Business
  • Bank Account Number and IFSC Code
  • Main business activity (Manufacturing or Service)
  • Details of investment in Plant & Machinery/Equipment
  • Details of Turnover
