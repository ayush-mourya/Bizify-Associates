FSSAI Food License Registration

Any business involved in the manufacturing, processing, storage, distribution, or sale of food products in India is required to obtain a food license from the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). Bizify Associates simplifies the FSSAI registration process, whether it's Basic Registration, State License, or Central License, depending on your business turnover and nature of operations. We ensure your food business operates legally and maintains food safety standards.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of the applicant
  • Proof of business premises (Rent Agreement, Electricity Bill)
  • Nature of business and list of food products
  • Partnership Deed/MOA/AOA (for company/LLP/partnership)
  • Layout plan of the processing unit (for manufacturing)
  • List of machinery and equipment (for manufacturing)
  • NOC from local municipality (if required)
