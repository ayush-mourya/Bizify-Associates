Fire NOC Acquisition Services

A Fire No Objection Certificate (NOC) is mandatory for various establishments like commercial buildings, factories, hospitals, educational institutions, and residential complexes to ensure they meet fire safety standards. Bizify Associates assists in obtaining Fire NOCs from the respective Fire Departments. Our service includes guiding through the installation of fire safety measures, preparing necessary documentation, and facilitating inspections to ensure compliance and safety.

Documents Required (Varies by State/Building Type)

  • Building Plan (approved by competent authority)
  • Fire Safety Plan/Evacuation Plan
  • Details of fire fighting equipment installed
  • Site Plan and complete address of the premises
  • Proof of ownership/occupancy of the building
  • Latest property tax receipt
  • NOC from other relevant departments (if applicable)
