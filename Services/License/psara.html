PSARA License for Private Security Agencies

The Private Security Agencies (Regulation) Act, 2005 (PSARA) governs the functioning of private security agencies in India, making it mandatory for them to obtain a license to operate legally. Bizify Associates provides comprehensive assistance in acquiring the PSARA license, covering documentation, police verification, training certification, and other state-specific requirements. We ensure your security agency is fully compliant, enabling you to conduct operations smoothly and legitimately.

Documents Required (Varies by State)

  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of Directors/Partners/Proprietor
  • Proof of business address (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement)
  • Certificate of Incorporation/Registration
  • Memorandum of Association & Articles of Association
  • Training certificate for directors/employees
  • Affidavit regarding criminal record/police verification
  • Details of security guards and supervisors
  • Balance Sheet & P&L Statement of the company
