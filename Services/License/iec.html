Import Export Code (IEC) Registration

The Import Export Code (IEC) is a mandatory 10-digit code issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) for all businesses involved in import and export activities in India. Bizify Associates assists in obtaining your IEC, a lifetime registration that simplifies international trade and is crucial for customs clearance and availing export/import benefits.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the applicant entity (individual, company, firm)
  • Applicant's Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID
  • Proof of address of the establishment (Rent Agreement/Utility Bill)
  • Bank Certificate or cancelled cheque of the current account
  • Certificate of Incorporation/Registration Certificate (for company/LLP/firm)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) - Class 2 or 3
  • Details of nature of business (e.g., manufacturer, merchant exporter)
