Liquor License Acquisition

Obtaining a liquor license is a complex process with stringent regulations that vary significantly by state in India. It's essential for restaurants, bars, hotels, and retail outlets wishing to serve or sell alcoholic beverages. Bizify Associates provides expert guidance and support through the entire application process, from understanding the specific requirements of your state's excise department to preparing and submitting all necessary documentation, ensuring a compliant and timely acquisition of your liquor license.

Documents Required (Highly state-specific)

  • Identity and Address proof of applicant/partners/directors
  • Proof of ownership/lease of the premises
  • Building plan/site plan
  • No Objection Certificates (NOCs) from relevant authorities (e.g., Fire, Police, Municipality)
  • Business registration documents (Company/LLP/Partnership Deed)
  • Financial statements of the applicant
  • Details of past criminal records (if any)
