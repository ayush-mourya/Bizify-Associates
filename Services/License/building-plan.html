Building Plan Approval & Sanction

Obtaining a building plan approval and sanction from the relevant municipal corporation or local authority is a mandatory legal requirement before commencing any construction or renovation project. This ensures that the proposed structure adheres to building codes, zoning regulations, and safety standards. Bizify Associates assists individuals and businesses in preparing and submitting all necessary documents and drawings for expedited approval.

Documents Required

  • Application form (duly filled and signed)
  • Ownership documents of the plot/land (Sale Deed, Lease Deed)
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt
  • Architectural drawings (site plan, floor plans, elevations, sections)
  • Structural drawings and stability certificate (if applicable)
  • No Objection Certificates (NOCs) from relevant departments (e.g., Fire, Airport Authority)
  • Area statement and calculation sheet
  • Copy of PAN and Aadhaar of applicant/owner
  • Photographs of the site
