Pollution Control Board (PCB) Consent

Businesses engaged in manufacturing processes, industries, or activities that may cause air, water, or noise pollution are required to obtain Consent to Establish (CTE) and Consent to Operate (CTO) from their respective State Pollution Control Boards (SPCB) or Pollution Control Committees (PCC). Bizify Associates assists in navigating the complex environmental regulations, preparing applications, and securing these essential clearances for sustainable and compliant operations.

Documents Required

  • Application Form (as per SPCB/PCC norms)
  • Company/Unit Registration Certificates (e.g., MoA/AoA, Partnership Deed)
  • PAN and Aadhaar of Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Land ownership documents/Lease Deed
  • Site plan/Layout plan
  • Detailed Project Report (DPR)
  • Manufacturing process flow chart
  • Raw material details and finished product details
  • Details of water consumption, wastewater generation, and treatment plans (ETP/STP)
  • Details of air emissions and pollution control measures (APC devices)
  • Details of hazardous and solid waste generation and disposal methods
  • Consent fee payment receipt
  • Any existing environmental clearances or permits
